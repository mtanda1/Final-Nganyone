What is Information Science?

"We are a STEM bachelor's program within the College of Information Studies that provides students with the skills to anticipate consequences of new information technologies, systems, services and resources. We learn technical skills such as database design, information architecture, web and mobile development, and data analytics combined with social sciences, leadership, design, and humanities. "

More information can be found on the Ischool website!

About the Information Science Society

Our aim is to form a community that helps students (especially infosci students!) build and expand their professional network, learn about modern technologies, and meet students with similar interests. We hold events throughout the semester that are fun and engaging and we encourage everyone to come!
